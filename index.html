What this is

  • No clearnet leash

    Lives on Tor and I2P first. Clearnet is not a target, it's a fallback at best.

  • Free speech absolutist

    Moderation is per-room, not platform-wide. No global thought police.

  • Niche by design

    Built for miscellaneous hidden services. Not Discord. Not Matrix. Not Revolt.

  • Self-hostable

    Run your own node behind your own onion or eepsite. Federate if you want.

Getting in

  1. Use Tor Browser or an I2P router. No clearnet bridge.
  2. Open the .onion or eepsite address above.
  3. Generate a local keypair in-client. No email, no phone, no recovery.
  4. Pick a room. Lurk first. Don't be a tourist.
